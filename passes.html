To make the most of your ski vacation, finding the best deals on lift passes and ski packages is key.

Easily plan your ideal ski trip from home with the help of professionals

Season Passes

For avid skiers who plan to hit the slopes frequently throughout the winter, season passes offer the most cost-effective option.

  • Epic Pass: Access to over 40 resorts globally, including Vail and Whistler Blackcomb. ~$949 for adults, ~$489 for children.
  • Ikon Pass: Covers 50+ resorts such as Aspen, Jackson Hole, and Mammoth. ~$1,179 for adults, ~$339 for children.
  • Benefits: Unlimited skiing at select resorts, discounts on lodging, rentals, and food.
  • Early Bird Discounts: Purchase early for savings of up to 20%.

Multi-Day Passes

Ideal for skiers who want to enjoy multiple days on the slopes without committing to a full season, these passes offer flexibility and savings.

  • Vail Resorts Multi-Day Pass: Can be used for 3 to 7 days at resorts like Breckenridge and Park City. ~$375 for 3 days, ~$650 for 7 days.
  • Benefits: Cheaper than single-day tickets, flexible usage across the season.
  • Savings: 15-30% off single-day prices when bought in advance.

Local Deals and Packages

These bundled deals combine lift passes, lodging, and sometimes rentals or lessons, offering great value for vacationers.

  • Ski-and-Stay Package: Includes hotel accommodations and lift tickets at resorts like Stowe or Sun Valley. Starting at ~$150 per night with lift tickets included.
  • Perks: Extra discounts on rentals, meals, and other amenities.
  • Advance Booking: Save up to 25% by booking months ahead.

Group Discounts

For families or friends traveling together, group discounts can make skiing more affordable with special rates for larger parties.

  • Group Skiing Discounts: Available for groups of 10 or more at resorts like Big Sky or Breckenridge. Starting at ~$85 per day for group members.
  • Benefits: Lower ticket prices and sometimes discounted rentals and lessons.
  • Booking Tip: Reserve early to secure group discounts during peak season.

Off-Peak Pricing

Skiing during off-peak times, like weekdays or non-holiday periods, offers lower ticket prices and less crowded slopes.

  • Off-Peak Lift Tickets: Resorts like Alta and Sun Valley offer reduced prices during the week. Starting at ~$90 per day (compared to ~$150 on weekends).
  • Benefits: Save 30-40% on tickets, fewer crowds, and more availability for lessons.

Kids and Senior Passes

Many resorts provide special discounts for children, teenagers, and seniors, making family trips more budget-friendly.

  • Kids Ski Free: Resorts like Snowmass and Deer Valley offer free passes for kids under 5 or 6. Free for kids (with the purchase of an adult pass).
  • Senior Discounts: Seniors (65+) can access cheaper tickets at resorts like Aspen or Jackson Hole. Starting at ~$80 per day.
  • Family Savings: Multi-day family packages provide additional savings when booked together.
