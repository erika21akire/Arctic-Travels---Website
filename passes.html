To make the most of your ski vacation, finding the best deals on lift passes and ski packages is key.

Easily plan your ideal ski trip from home with the help of professionals

Season Passes

For avid skiers who plan to hit the slopes frequently.

Epic Pass:

Access to over 40 resorts globally including Vail and Whistler Blackcomb.

Price: ~$949 (Adults), ~$489 (Children)

Ikon Pass:

Access to 50+ resorts like Aspen and Mammoth.

Price: ~$1,179 (Adults), ~$339 (Children)

Benefits: Unlimited skiing, lodging and rental discounts.

Early Bird: Save up to 20%!

Multi-Day Passes

Ideal for skiers who want multiple days on the slopes.

Vail Resorts Multi-Day Pass:

Valid for 3 to 7 days at Breckenridge and Park City.

Price: ~$375 (3 days), ~$650 (7 days)

Benefits: Save 15-30% on tickets bought in advance.

Local Deals and Packages

Bundled deals combining lift passes, lodging, and rentals.

Ski-and-Stay Package:

Includes hotel and lift tickets at resorts like Stowe or Sun Valley.

Price: Starting at ~$150 per night with tickets.

Benefits: Discounts on rentals, meals, and amenities.

Group Discounts

Discounted rates for larger groups.

Group Skiing Discounts:

Available for groups of 10+ at Big Sky and Breckenridge.

Price: Starting at ~$85 per day per person.

Benefits: Discounted rentals and lessons.

Off-Peak Pricing

Lower prices during weekdays or non-holiday periods.

Off-Peak Lift Tickets:

Alta and Sun Valley offer reduced prices mid-week.

Price: Starting at ~$90 per day.

Benefits: Save 30-40%, fewer crowds.

Kids and Senior Passes

Special discounts for children, teenagers, and seniors.

Kids Ski Free:

Free passes for kids under 5 or 6 at Snowmass and Deer Valley.

Senior Discounts: Cheaper tickets for seniors (65+).

Price: Starting at ~$80 per day.

Family Packages: Save more with multi-day family deals.
